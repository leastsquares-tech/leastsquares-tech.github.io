About

We draw the diagrams ML/AI papers forget to include.

Least Squares is a content studio focused on ML/AI architectures, system design, and ML design. We translate dense papers, blog posts, and production lore into annotated, whiteboard-style visuals that engineers can actually use.

We're not a course platform. We're a publication — daily snippets on Instagram and LinkedIn, longer breakdowns on YouTube, and visual essays here.

If you build, train, deploy, or just think hard about intelligent systems — you're the reader we're writing for.
